Scores come from a Bradley-Terry model fit on pairwise LLM rankings of all Inkhaven posts. Each post's σ-score is its BT log-strength divided by the dataset standard deviation; 95% CIs are bootstrap percentiles across resampled ranking groups. P(top 20%) is the bootstrap frequency of the post landing in the top 20% across resamples.
